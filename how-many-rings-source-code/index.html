How to play

Roll

Each round gives you one random franchise and era. Pick one player from that board for your rotation.

Build 6

Fill PG, SG, SF, PF, C, and 6th man. Use the position filters to find the slot you need.

Use skips

You get one team skip and one era skip per draft. Weaker eras show up more often, so use them carefully.

Run dynasty

After six picks, simulate 10 years. Your result is how many rings and Finals trips the team wins.
